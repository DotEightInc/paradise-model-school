Our Story

Paradise Model School was established in the year 2000.

PARADISE SCHOOL formerly Future Hope Nusery and Primary School started September 18, 2000. The name was later changed to PARADISE MODEL SCHOOL on October 8, 2001, when the school was offically registered with the Ogun State Ministry of Education, Science and Technology.
